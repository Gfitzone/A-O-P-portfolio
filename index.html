About Me

I am a dedicated Second Lieutenant in the Nepal Army, committed to serving my country and upholding its values. With a passion for leadership and a strong sense of duty, I have undergone rigorous training to prepare for the challenges of military life. I graduated from the Nepalese Military Academy, where I developed my skills in strategy, tactics, and teamwork. My experiences in various training exercises and operations have taught me the importance of discipline, resilience, and adaptability in high-pressure situations. Beyond my military duties, I am deeply invested in the well-being of my community. I believe in the importance of social responsibility and often engage in initiatives that support local development and disaster relief efforts. In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities, including hiking and trekking, which allow me to connect with Nepal's beautiful landscape. I am also an avid reader, continually seeking knowledge to enhance my understanding of leadership and military history. I am proud to serve in the Nepal Army and am committed to contributing positively to my country and its future.
